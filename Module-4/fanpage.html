Bio

Lemon Demon is a musical project by Neil Cicierega. Most of the projects music is preformed solely my Cicierega. The bands most recent ablum Spirit Phone, released in 2016 received an 8/10 from youtube critic Anthony Fantano.
The album was the #1 best-selling album on Bandcamp for the first week of its release.
